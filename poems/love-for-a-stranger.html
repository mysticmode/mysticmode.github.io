Love for a stranger [2022-12-10]

My dear strange love
I wanted to go wherever you go now;

Sounds a bit haphazard ask, right?
Yeah!, but you know, those unmade paths
that you are gonna take me are gonna be
the whistling moments that gets me
out of this sluggish reality;

Is that the life that puts me on thorn foots or I'm on
a phase where me going nowhere, I'm no sure of either;

All I can feel now is the beats that gets me on you,
is right on with you as well;

My dear strange love,
don't you get it? I'm meant to this strange moment
that you are the one who's gonna take me and take me away.
