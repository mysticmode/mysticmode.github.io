Seeker [2022-10-14]

We are a seeking creature
soulfully in and out;
The seeker arrives only when you
break yourself apart from this outwardly
structure of our society.

The soul doesn’t see the mirage, it is your
illusionary mind for which the truth is only
the means of survival.

We are the angels in this earthly
kingdom, not alone but belonging together with our
celestial tribes in harmony. By realizing this
we go to the deep roots of our cosmic nature.

So, do not hesitate to go beyond the boundaries,
for which your seeking soul wakes up.
